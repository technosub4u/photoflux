AI Content Standards

Built-in Content Filtering

PhotoFlux AI incorporates advanced AI content filtering to ensure safe and appropriate content generation:

  • Automatic Content Moderation: Built-in safety mechanisms prevent generation of inappropriate, harmful, or offensive content.
  • Real-time Filtering: All AI-generated images are processed through content safety filters before display.
  • Industry Standards: Filtering adheres to industry-standard AI safety guidelines and best practices.

Acceptable Use Policy

Permitted Uses:

  • Creating artistic and creative content
  • Enhancing personal photographs
  • Restoring old or damaged photos
  • Educational and professional projects
  • Social media content creation

Prohibited Uses:

  • Generating harmful, offensive, or inappropriate content
  • Creating misleading or deceptive imagery
  • Violating intellectual property rights
  • Promoting violence or hate
  • Generating explicit or adult content
